About Licious Mutton

There is nothing more tempting than a hot plate of delicious Mutton Masala or Hyderabadi Biryani. Mutton dishes are an integral part of Indian cuisine, with the flavoursome and tender meat being sourced from a lamb or a goat. As compared to other types of red meat, mutton contains a lower level of cholesterol and fat.

Mutton is used in mouth-watering delicacies like galouti kabab, mutton biryani, rogan josh, dum style mutton, seekh kebabs, keema and more. Not just contained to Indian dishes, continental meaty meals like lamb chops, lamb lasagne and spaghetti bolognaise are also mutton favorites. Loaded with essential nutrients like proteins and minerals, making mutton a part of your balanced diet is a great way to enjoy a hearty meal that’s packed with protein and flavour.

At Licious, we love our mutton just as much as you do. So, we only give you the freshest and meatiest cuts of goat and lamb. Our experts carefully select the goats and lambs and practise artisanal butchery. Every portion is quality controlled, so no matter which cut you pick, rest assured it is the freshest, safest and of highest quality.
